what is playground?
a series of experimental music tools.
no manuals. no friction. just play.

each release follows industrial design nomenclature:
mk-1, mk-2, mk-3...

if a beginner needs more than 5 seconds to understand it,
it doesn't belong here.
values:
  • accessibility first
  • experimentation over perfection
  • learn by doing
  • no gatekeeping
